You feel your skin prickle and a sense of apprehension, as in the moment before a long-gathering storm breaks. The feeling intensifies sharply until it suddenly snaps off and you realise that you're not standing *anywhere*. Your mouth, if you could be sure of having one at this point, would be full of the taste of tin.
A tiny, brisk voice says 'please wait while you are transferred' and muzak begins to play where you imagine the base of your skull would be.
